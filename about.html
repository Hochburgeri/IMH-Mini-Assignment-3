Fun Facts About Lil Nas X
  1. Lil Nas X’s real name is Montero Lamar Hill. Hill’s stage Name “Lil Nas X” was a tribute to rapper “Nas”.

  2. Lil Nas X is 22 years old. He was born on April 9, 1999, in Lithia Springs, Georgia.
  3. Lil Nas X is 6 feet 1 inch (186 cm) in height.
  4. On December 3, 2018, Lil Nas went viral on TikTok with his country rap song titled “Old Town Road”.
  5. Old Town Road became the first hip hop song to sit at number one in the charts for 13 consecutive weeks.
  6. He was the first artist to have a song remain at number one on the Billboard 100 list for 19 weeks.
  7. In 2019, on the last day of Pride Month in the USA, Lil Nas X came out publicly as being gay by asking his followers to listen carefully to his latest single “c7osure”.
  8. In January 2020, Lil Nas X won two Grammy awards; Best Music Video and Best Pop Duo Performance with Billy Ray Cyrus.
